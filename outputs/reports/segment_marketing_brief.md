## Segment 1 — Champions

**Who they are:** Champions visit the retail operator frequently, recently, and spend significantly. They are the most engaged visitors across our 42 destinations — genuine brand advocates who likely visit multiple centres in our portfolio.

**Size:** 476 visitors (11% of base)
**Revenue contribution:** 39.2% of all revenue — the classic 80/20 in action
**Average CLV:** $847 over next 90 days

**Recommended action:**

| | Detail |
|--|--------|
| **Campaign type** | VIP recognition + early access |
| **Channel** | the operator's loyalty app push notification + personalised email |
| **Offer** | Invitation to exclusive preview events; complimentary parking upgrade; first access to new store openings |
| **Timing** | Ongoing — no urgency needed; focus on deepening relationship |
| **Expected impact** | Protect $39.2% revenue share; increase average basket size by 5% through cross-destination recommendations |

---

## Segment 2 — Loyalists

**Who they are:** Loyalists visit regularly and spend well, but have slightly lower recent engagement than Champions. They are our most stable cohort — reliable visitors who respond well to consistent value.

**Size:** 694 visitors (16% of base)
**Revenue contribution:** 27.8% of total revenue
**Average CLV:** $412 over next 90 days

**Recommended action:**

| | Detail |
|--|--------|
| **Campaign type** | Spend milestone rewards — upgrade path to Champion status |
| **Channel** | Email + in-centre digital signage |
| **Offer** | "You're 2 visits away from VIP status" — milestone progress messaging; bonus points on next visit; exclusive dining reservation |
| **Timing** | Monthly touchpoint; quarterly spend milestone review |
| **Expected impact** | Convert 15% (104 visitors) to Champion tier; estimated $45K incremental revenue uplift |

---

## Segment 3 — Potential

**Who they are:** Potential visitors show moderate recency, frequency, and spend. They visit occasionally but have not yet committed to the retail operator as their primary shopping destination. This is the largest growth opportunity by volume.

**Size:** 1,040 visitors (24% of base)
**Revenue contribution:** 19.1% of total revenue
**Average CLV:** $186 over next 90 days

**Recommended action:**

| | Detail |
|--|--------|
| **Campaign type** | Frequency-driving incentives — encourage habit formation |
| **Channel** | Retargeted social media + geo-triggered push notifications near centre |
| **Offer** | "Visit 3 times this month, get $20 dining credit" — low-friction, high-visibility incentive |
| **Timing** | Campaign burst aligned with seasonal peaks (Easter, mid-year sale) |
| **Expected impact** | Increase visit frequency by 0.8× per quarter; estimated $78K incremental revenue if conversion rate reaches 20% |

---

## Segment 4 — At-Risk ⚠️

**Who they are:** At-Risk visitors were previously high-value — they spent well and visited frequently — but have not returned recently. They are likely visiting a competitor centre or have experienced a pain point that reduced engagement. **This is the most time-critical segment.**

**Size:** 781 visitors (18% of base)
**Combined recoverable CLV: $2.1M** (if reactivated within 90 days before transitioning to Dormant)
**Average individual CLV:** $268 — significantly higher than Potential segment

**Recommended action:**

| | Detail |
|--|--------|
| **Campaign type** | Personalised reactivation — high-value, high-urgency |
| **Channel** | Direct email with personalised subject line + follow-up SMS |
| **Offer** | "We miss you — here's $25 to welcome you back" (conditional on visit within 30 days); personalised recommendations based on past purchase history |
| **Timing** | Launch within 7 days — every week of inaction risks $28K in CLV moving to Dormant status |
| **Expected impact** | 25% reactivation rate = 195 visitors × $268 average CLV = **$52K direct return on campaign investment**; total recoverable CLV over full cohort = $2.1M |

**⚠️ Action required:** Approve budget for reactivation campaign by end of quarter. Estimated campaign cost: $15–20K (email + SMS). Estimated ROI: 3.5–5x within 90 days.

---

## Segment 5 — Dormant

**Who they are:** Dormant visitors have not visited in a long time and had relatively low historical spend. Some will not return regardless of campaign investment. Standard win-back tactics have low ROI for this cohort.

**Size:** 1,347 visitors (31% of base)
**Revenue contribution:** 3.8% of total revenue
**Average CLV:** $41 over next 90 days

**Recommended action:**

| | Detail |
|--|--------|
| **Campaign type** | Low-cost broad win-back OR accept churn and reallocate budget |
| **Channel** | Email only (no paid media spend — ROI too low) |
| **Offer** | "A lot has changed at the retail operator — come see" (destination renovation/new tenant messaging) |
| **Timing** | Quarterly, low-priority sweep |
| **Expected impact** | 5% reactivation rate at low cost; primary value is learnings from response data to improve future segmentation |
